No-Bake Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies


Description

These no-bake chocolate oatmeal cookies are a quick and easy treat perfect for hot summer days when you don’t want to turn on the oven. Made with pantry staples like oats, peanut butter, and cocoa powder, they come together in minutes and set up beautifully in the fridge. With thousands of five-star reviews, these cookies are a guaranteed crowd-pleaser for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine sugar, milk, butter, and cocoa. Bring to a boil and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in oats, peanut butter, and vanilla until well combined.
  4. Drop spoonfuls onto waxed paper and allow to cool and harden, about 30 minutes.
